Kimchi Fried Rice

Description

Fried Rice with Kimchi

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • Â¼ cup chopped bacon
  • 1 green onion, sliced, white and green parts separated
  • 1 cup kimchi, drained and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste), or to taste (Optional)
  • 3 cups cooked short-grain rice
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 teaspoon butter
  • 1 egg

Directions

  1. Heat canola oil in large skillet over high heat. Stir bacon and white parts of green onion into skillet and reduce heat to medium. Cook and stir until bacon is cooked and onion is fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
  2. Stir kimchi and gochujang into mixture and cook until warmed through and fragrant, 2 to 4 minutes. Add rice; cook and stir until rice is heated through and coated with gochujang, 3 to 5 minutes more.
  3. Drizzle sesame oil over rice mixture and stir to coat. Garnish with green parts of green onion.
  4. Melt 1 teaspoon butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Crack egg into the pan and cook until white is completely set and yolk is thick, 3 to 4 minutes. Place egg over fried rice.
