Chicken Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

Chicken, mushroom and pasta in a creamy garlic and cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces fettuccini pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into chunks
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups heavy cream
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat butter in a large skillet and brown chicken and mushrooms until chicken is cooked through. Season with garlic salt and pepper. Add whipping cream and cook until thick, stirring constantly.
  3. Add Parmesan cheese when at desired consistency. Serve over noodles.
